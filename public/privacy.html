Privacy Policy

A&B Home Improvement LLC · Last updated: July 4, 2026

A&B Home Improvement LLC ("A&B," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, how we handle text messaging, and your choices.

Information we collect

Details you submit through our website forms: your name, phone number, email, property address, and the project or service you're interested in. Payment information is processed securely by our payment provider (Stripe); we do not store your card number.

How we use your information

To respond to your quote or inspection request, schedule and provide services, contact you by phone, email, or text about your inquiry, appointment, estimate, or payment, and to process payments you authorize.

SMS / text messaging

If you provide your mobile number, we may text you about your request, appointment, estimate, or payment. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help.

No mobile information — including your phone number or your consent to receive text messages — will be shared with or sold to any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Who we share information with

We use trusted service providers only to operate our business — website form delivery (Formspree), payment processing (Stripe), text messaging (Twilio), and secure data storage (Supabase). We never sell your personal information, and we never share it for third-party marketing.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to serve you and meet legal requirements, and we protect it with reasonable safeguards.

Your choices

Reply STOP to opt out of texts at any time, or contact us to update or delete your information.
